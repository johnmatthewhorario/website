About Me

Hello, I am John Matthew Horario, your SK Chairman. It is my honor to serve the youth of our community and represent your voices in local governance.

Role and Responsibilities

As an SK Chairman, I am responsible for:

  • Advocating for the needs and interests of the youth.
  • Implementing programs and projects for the development of the youth sector.
  • Representing the SK in barangay and municipal/city council meetings.
  • Managing the SK budget and resources transparently and accountably.
